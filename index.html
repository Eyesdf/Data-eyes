Instructions

  1. Press the start button.
  2. Position yourself in front of the camera (preferably use back camera).
  3. Focus your eye on the camera.
  4. Keep a distance of ~10–15 cm.
  5. Wait approximately 10 seconds until the software detects the eye (it will draw a box around the eye).
  6. Open your eye wide and hold still.
  7. When you hear the beep, press “Capture”.
  8. Take two photos, wait 2 seconds between each one.
  9. Make sure the eye is centered in the image.
